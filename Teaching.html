Teaching

Semantic Web Module

Together with the Intelligent Systems department at Uni Leipzig, AKSW created the master module Semantic Web. The module is usually held in the summer semester and consists of a lecture, a seminar and practical work.

Module component-based software engineering

“Software aus Komponenten”

Together with the Intelligent Systems department at Uni Leipzig, AKSW created the master module “Software aus Komponenten”. The module is usually held in the winter semester and consists of a lecture, and a seminar.

SlideWiki

AKSW develops the collaborative e-learning content authoring tool SlideWiki. With SlideWiki educators and students can work together on creating richly structured e-learning content such as presentations and self-assessment tests. Further information about SlideWiki can be found in the SlideWiki documentation.

Student Supervision

AKSW actively supervises internships as well as bachelor and master thesis. Most of the thesis topics are related to Web technologies, Semantic Web, ontologies, information systems and data management. They are usually performed in the context of an AKSW Project. You can also have a look at BIS Abschlussarbeiten.

Leipzig School of Media

AKSW organizes and performs the master study course Content- & Media-Engineering at Leipzig School of Media.
