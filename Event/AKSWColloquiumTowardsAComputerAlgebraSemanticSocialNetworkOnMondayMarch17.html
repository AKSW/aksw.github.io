AKSW Colloquium “Towards a Computer Algebra Semantic Social Network” on Monday, March 17

On Monday, March 17th, 2014 at 1.30 p.m. in Room P702 (Paulinum), Prof. Dr. Hans-Gert Gräbe will present and discuss a bootstrap process for a Semantic Social Network within a special scientific community based on enhancements of xodx.

For the moment, they operate a SPARQL endpoint and extract valuable information to the WordPress based website of the German CA-Fachgruppe.

As always, Bachelor and Master students are able to get points for attendance and there is complimentary coffee and cake after the session.
