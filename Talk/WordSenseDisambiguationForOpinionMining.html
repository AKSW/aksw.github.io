Word Sense Disambiguation for opinion mining.

The abundance of information available on the Web raises the need for techniques that are able to analyze and make a better use of such huge information. Investigating people’s opinions play a major and active role in making crucial decisions in order to improve the performance and to avoid deficiencies. Word Sense Disambiguation (WSD) is one of the essential problems to opinion mining or also called sentiment analysis. In this work we provide a solution to the WSD problem by combining two important techniques which are the semantic Web technology and the opinion mining and sentiment analysis techniques. Sentiment analysis extracts people’s opinions in an automatic manner, while semantic Web technology provides a useful improvement by enriching data with additional meaning and make data better understood by human and machines as well.
