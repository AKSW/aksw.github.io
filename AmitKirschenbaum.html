About

I am a research associate at the Knowledge Integration and Linked Data Technologies (KILT) group, where I combine my interests in Natural Language Processing and Semantic Web technologies.
My research includes:

  • computational morphology
  • concept linking
  • language typology and documentation.

I enjoy transforming raw data into useful information, and have been involved as a researcher in various projects concerned with language processing, by machines or humans, ranging from machine translation to psycholinguistics.
