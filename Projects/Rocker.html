Rocker

http://aksw.org/Projects/Rocker
Abstract:
Rocker is a refinement-operator-based approach for the extraction of keys and almost-keys from RDF datasets. Besides being finite, proper and non-redundant, Rocker is a state-of-the-art tool which can scale to very large knowledge bases.
