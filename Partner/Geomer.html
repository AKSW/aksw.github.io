geomer

geomer GmbH considers itself as a committed provider of geo-intelligence solutions for challenging geographical tasks. geomer is an independent company with private shareholders. The company was founded with domicile in Heidelberg in 1999. With a scientific background and due to the integrative mastering of different competence fields, geomer provides its customers with above-average solution excellence - from the consultancy to the IT technical implementation. The focuses of our activities are products, solutions and project services in the areas of geo-intelligence, geo-spatial data, risk provisioning and natural hazards.
