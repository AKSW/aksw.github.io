Piranha womex AG (D)

Made in Berlin since 1987, piranha womex is an independent creative company which combines music and IT to increase the value of crossing borders, be these musical, political, religious, cultural or commercial. Our inhouse experts have developed a dynamic portfolio which at present includes international music networking events such as WOMEX and Classical:NEXT ; worldwide consultancies such as Porto Musical and the AME Atlantic Music Expo; IT projects such as SyncGlobal, a cooperation with the MP3-inventor Frauenhofer IDMT; and the Piranha Musik label & publishing, introducing new sounds & artists from the world to the world.
